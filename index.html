MiHoYo

miHoYo is one of China's leading video game development companies, founded in 2012 with the famous slogan "Tech Otakus Save the World". The company has always been a pioneer in applying advanced graphics technology to create vivid virtual worlds, strongly reflecting the distinctive anime style and attracting millions of fans worldwide.

MiHoYo Games

MiHoYo's popular titles such as Genshin Impact, Honkai: Star Rail, and Zenless Zone Zero always impress with their captivating role-playing gameplay and deep storylines. Featuring stunning cel-shading 3D graphics and smooth combat mechanics, these games not only provide top-notch entertainment but also open up incredibly epic open-world adventures for gamers.
